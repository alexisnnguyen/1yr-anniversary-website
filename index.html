Best way to start off the new school year (your last at USC too). Such a different experience compared to the first time I went to LA, but this was unique in that I got a better glimpse at YOUR life.

Seattle | Portland

That short, little over 24 hour trip to Seattle for my birthday was absolutely incredible given the amount of time we had. I want to thank you for going out of your way to fly up here, and I want you to know I appreciate all my time with you from our Whiskey Barrel-Aged Cold Brew to the Space needle and the Chihuly garden to the super duper fancy birthday dinner.

Staycation in Portland

Portland

Brownies, pillow forts, pasta, and other at-home fall activities made this the cutest experience yet. It gave me a glimpse of how life could be if we were no longer long distance. Felt like I was high on cloud 9 (literally).

The return to LA

December 2023

Los Angeles | Mammoth Mountain

So much happened during this trip, where do I even begin... I couldn't have asked for a better way to end my, already so memorable, 2023. It was so great we even had allergic reactions on the last day.

  • The Getty, Malibu, and our road trip along the pacific coast highway was something out of a movie
  • I learned to ice skate thanks to my amazing and handsome teacher
  • John Summit at BMO was iconic
  • To add on to the learning winter sports theme, I learned to snowboard at Mammoth and ate shit (still mad at you for making me go on a blue TWICE during the snow storm)
